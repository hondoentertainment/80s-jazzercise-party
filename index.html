The Dress Code

What does Cowboy Disco look like?

Think barn meets mirror ball — boots, fringe, rhinestones, and zero shame. Denim, sequins, cowboy hats, and disco glam. If it sparkles under the lights, you're doing it right.

  • Boots + fringe = chef's kiss
  • Sequins and rhinestones in any shade — gold, silver, or electric blue
  • Cowboy hats, belt buckles, and satin shirts welcome
  • Denim on denim — mandatory vibes

FAQ

Quick answers before you hit the dance floor.

How do I invite someone up?

At the Woodlawn entrance, find K. Henderson in the directory and call — Kyle will buzz them in. Enter through the gate (door on the left), then take the stairs or elevator to the 5th floor and look for apartment 327 / Cowboy Disco Saloon.

Where are the bathrooms?

Bathrooms are located in the lobby and in apartment 327.

How do I vote for best outfit?

Hosts hand out numbered tags at the door. Enter that contestant number on the Best Outfit Vote page before voting closes.

Are there prizes for best outfit?

Yes — there are prizes for the best outfit. Vote by contestant number before voting closes. Bring your A-game fringe and sequins.

Can Kyle and Jeremy help plan my next party?

They might! Kyle and Jeremy can help plan your next party — if you ask them nice.

What about food allergies?

Just ask! Any of the cooks are happy to tell you what's in the food so you can eat safely.

When is the next party?

We're still deciding! Visit our poll to vote on when it should be held and give us feedback on how this one went.
