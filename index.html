The Dress Code

What does 80s Jazzercise look like?

Think Richard Simmons energy — hot pink, high energy, and zero shame. Leotards, leg warmers, headbands, and neon everything. If it glows under black light, you're doing it right.

  • Leg warmers + leotard = chef's kiss
  • Neon spandex in any shade — pink, purple, or highlighter yellow
  • Sweatbands, scrunchies, and oversized graphic tees welcome
  • White sneakers with tall socks — mandatory vibes

FAQ

Quick answers before you break a sweat.

How do I invite someone up?

Have your guest buzz Kyle (apartment 327) at the Woodlawn entrance and we'll let them in.

Where are the bathrooms?

Bathrooms are located in the lobby and in apartment 327.

How do I vote for best outfit?

Hosts hand out numbered tags at the door. Enter that contestant number on the Best Outfit Vote page before voting closes.

Are there prizes for best outfit?

Yes — there are prizes for the best outfit. Vote by contestant number before voting closes. Bring your A-game spandex.

Can Kyle and Jeremy help plan my next party?

They might! Kyle and Jeremy can help plan your next party — if you ask them nice.

What about food allergies?

Just ask! Any of the cooks are happy to tell you what's in the food so you can eat safely.
